Who We Are

Refining clarity,
Defining space.

DGS (Decor Glass & Solutions) is a professional glass contractor company specializing in all types of glass work. Established in 2012, we have been delivering pan India high-quality glass solutions for both interior and exterior projects across residential, commercial, and industrial spaces.We Deal in all types of glass, Aluminum, ACP, MS & SS fabrication work in very competitive rates for Interior and exterior projects with branded quality materials excellent services on your door step.

We make all types op Doors, partition and windows, Shower cubicles, Railings, Sliding folding, Building facades, Glazing, Canopy, Domes,automatic sensor sliding door, wall concepts, Murals and much more with all types of fittings, installation and complete solutions.

Also we deal in Toughen glass, laminated glass, bend glass, annealed glass fabric & metal laminated glass, frosted glass, Decorative glass & mirrors,Backpainted glass, switchable glass, colored sheet glass, Figures sheet glass, magnetic glass, digital printed glass and all types of extra clear glass and extra clear mirrors.

Provide To Service ...Retail Fitout , Corporate Office , Residence project Interior ,Hotel And Hospitality Construction , MEP , HVAC , etc across all over india.

With a team of highly experienced and skilled professionals, including experts with years of hands-on knowledge in the glass industry, we are committed to providing premium craftsmanship and a smooth, hassle-free work experience for our clients. From customized glass creations to comprehensive installations, we handle every aspect of the project with precision and excellence. Whether it’s toughened glass, partitions, railings, mirrors, or decorative applications, we transform ideas into stunning realities.

At Decor Glass & Solutions, our mission is simple: to deliver lasting quality, functional elegance, and complete customer satisfaction in every project we undertake.
